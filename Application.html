Assistant General Manager, Production

On-site
DCP - Operations
Full time
Okpella, Edo, Nigeria
Posted 8 days ago

Job Description

The Assistant General Manager of Production at AutoHR will be responsible for monitoring and coordination of all activities related to the operation of the kiln and coal mills to achieve production and quality targets.

Key Responsibilities

  • Responsible for meeting production volumes budget for the year
  • Responsible for SHC, SPC and fuel mix as per budget
  • Ensure refractory work in kilns to have smooth and reliable run
  • Responsible for operational reliability of kilns and coal mills
  • Responsible for the implementation of all safety measures as required as per the company guidelines
  • Ensure production of high-quality Clinker in the plant in accordance with pre-set quality standards
  • Oversee the proper maintenance of the kiln to ensure kiln operations are executed effectively
  • Supervise the utilization of plant resources in the production unit operation, to ensure uninterrupted plant operations and employee/plant safety
  • Address production process bottlenecks for a better output, a greater stability of kiln operations and an improvement in environmental parameters
  • Oversee startups, shut down and operations in the kiln and section
  • Manage emergency situations to ensure adherence to standard procedures
  • Ensure process tests are conducted regularly to ensure that sensors in all lines function appropriately
  • Coordinate housekeeping activities at the kiln and CCR
  • Provide input to the development of the standard operating procedures for assigned production section
  • Ensure compliance to relevant ISO standards
  • Liaise with the shift Coordinator as necessary
  • Perform any other duties assigned by the GM, Production

Requirements

Educational Qualification:

  • Bachelor's degree or its equivalent in Engineering, Industrial Management, or a related field

Experience:

  • A minimum of 15 years of experience in production management, with at least 5 years in a senior management position in the manufacturing industry

Skills and Competencies:

  • In-depth knowledge of manufacturing processes and production management principles
  • Strong leadership and team management skills
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving abilities
  • Capacity for strategic thinking and planning
  • Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment and manage multiple priorities
  • Proficiency in production management software and Microsoft Office Suite
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
